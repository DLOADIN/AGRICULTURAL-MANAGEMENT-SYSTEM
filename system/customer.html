CONSUMER BEHAVIOR

Domestic Competitors: There's a noticeable trend towards specialty coffee consumption globally, driven by increasing consumer interest in unique flavor profiles, origin transparency, and sustainable production practices. Consumers are willing to pay premium prices for high-quality, ethically sourced coffee beans.

International Competitors: With busy lifestyles, there's a growing demand for convenient coffee solutions such as single-serve pods, ready-to-drink cold brews, and coffee capsules. Innovation in brewing methods and coffee-making equipment is also influencing consumer preferences.

Health and Wellness: Health and Concious consumers are seeking coffee options that offer functional benefits beyond caffeine stimulation. This includes products like low-acid coffee, Organic options and coffee blends with added nutrients or antioxidants

Diversification of Tea Products: Consumers are exploring a wide range of tea varieties beyond traditional black and green teas, including herbal infusions, flavored blends, and specialty teas like matcha and chai. This reflects a growing interest in diverse flavor experiences and cultural exploration

Health and Functional Benefits: Tea is increasingly perceived as a healthy beverage choice, with consumers drawn to its antioxidant properties, potential weight management benefits, and calming effects. Functional teas infused with herbs, spices, and botanicals are gaining popularity for their wellness benefits.

Sustainability and Ethical Sourcing:Consumers are placing greater emphasis on sustainability and ethical sourcing practices in the tea industry. They seek teas that are organic, fair trade certified, and produced using environmentally friendly cultivation methods

Rise in Demand for Fresh and Local Produce:Consumers are showing a preference for fresh,locally grown horticultural products including fruits, vegetables and herbs. This trend is driven by concerns over food safety, environmental Sustainability and supporting local economies.

Focus on Organic and Natural Products: There's a growing demand for organic horticultural products as consumers prioritize health and environmental Sustainability.Organic fruits, Vegetables and herbs are perceived as healthier and free from synthetic pesticides and chemicals.
