1.Production Trends:

-Cameroun's coffee production has shown resilience despite challenges with a focus on specialty varieties such as Arabica

- Efforts to modernize production techniques and expand cultivation ideas particularly in regions like the Western Highlands have contributed to steady yields

2.Consumption Patterns:

- Domestic coffee consumption is increasing, driven by urbanization, cafe culture and rising disposable incomes.

- There's a Growing preferences for specialty and gourmet coffee among urban customers, reflecting evolving tastes and lifestyles

3.Export Opprortunities:

- Cameroun has export Potential in the global coffee market with Opprortunities to supply specialty Arabica and Robust beans to international buyers

4.Challenges and Considerations:

- Challenges such as aging coffee trees, inconsistent quality and limited value addition hinder competitiveness in the global market.

- Investments in research, farmer education and processing infrastructure is crucial to address these challenges and enhance the quality and value proposition of Cameroun's coffee exports
