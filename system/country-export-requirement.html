COUNTRY EXPORT REQUIREMENTS

- Application letter to CEO(Objective and target commodity)

- Business certificate (or TIN for Individuals) indicating details on shareholders/membership and key business activities registered

-Business summary

-Business Profile(Vision, Mission, Objective, Product Specification, Physical Address and Place of Operation, targeted clients/markets).

-Business Specifications

-Business model

-Annual Production/ product supply plans for specific/ outgrowers scheme

-Proof of Investments made in production (Own farm, outgrowers scheme) to maintain a consistent supply

-Pricing (projected prices on target markets vs Production cost)

-Projections of quantity and value of export sales

-Valid Certificates issued by competent authorities and accreditated institutions for compliance with the requirements of a specific standard where applicable.

-Summarized Resumes(CVS) of Key technical stuff

-Contract and commitment letter of the designated for agricultural product handling, hygiene and sanitation or food safety


- Summarized Marketing strategy

- Existing buyer relationships

- Scope of business operations and activities - Organization structure with responsibilities of key staff in charge of operations and operations management

- Designated export handling facility with basic requirements, suitable for product handling, processing and storage.

- Description of equipment to ensure safety based on product Specifications where applicable.

- Process flow chart for the specific product for export

- List of suppliers approved by the company/cooperative

- Copies of premises/Farm/production sites registration certificate

- Copies of potential farmers/supplier's contracts
